Gummiberry Juice

Recipe:

  1. add 6 handful of red berries
  2. then 4 orange berries
  3. 3 purple berries
  4. 4 blue berries
  5. 3 green berries
  6. and only one yellow berry
  7. The recipe ends with the three-step-stir: First stir slowly to the right, then stir slowly to the left, then bang the pot to banish the bubbles.
